Dato' Lee Chong Wei

Legendary Badminton Icon of Malaysia

Dato' Lee Chong Wei's journey

  • 1982 - Born in Bagan Serai, Perak, Malaysia.

  • 1993 - Started learn badminton and discovered by Misbun Sidek, a former Malaysian professional badminton player.

  • 2000 - Drafted into national squad at 17 years old.

  • 2008 - Won his first silver medal in Olympic, becoming the Malaysian that reached the finals in the men's singles event.

  • 2019 - Lee announced his retirement after struggling to return to full fitness following a nose cancer diagnosis.

  • Lee's major achievements:
    • Holding the World No. 1 ranking for a total of 348 weeks.
    • Achieve 69 titles throughout the career.
    • Being a three-time Olympic silver medalist.
    • First non-Indonesian player to win 6 Indonesian Open titles.
